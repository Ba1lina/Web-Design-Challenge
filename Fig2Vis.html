City Latitude vs Humidity

Comparison

This scatter plot is analysing whether there is a relationship between the humidity of a city to its latitude. It seems that overall the sample cities have a humidity over 40% on the day the data is pulled. As of the date that the data was pulled on 3 May 2022 which is the summer in the Northern Hemisphere the data indicates that the cities in the Northwen Hemisphere between 0-40% are less humid.
